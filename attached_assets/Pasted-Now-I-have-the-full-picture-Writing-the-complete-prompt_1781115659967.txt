### Task 1 â€” Fix invisible text in TokenHighlightField multiline fields

**File**: `src/react/components/TokenHighlightField.tsx`

**What's happening**: `TokenHighlightField` is a two-layer component â€” a visible "backdrop" div that renders highlighted `{{token}}` spans, and a transparent `<textarea>` on top for cursor/caret interaction. Both layers share a `sharedLayer` style object (around line 493) that includes `overflow: 'hidden'`. When the textarea auto-grows (the `useLayoutEffect` sets `ta.style.height = ta.scrollHeight + 'px'`), the backdrop's `overflow: hidden` clips its visible text at the original container height, while the textarea's own text is `color: transparent` â€” making the content look white/invisible in the newly expanded area.

**Fix**: Remove `overflow: 'hidden'` from `sharedLayer` so it no longer applies to both layers indiscriminately. Instead:
- Apply `overflow: 'hidden'` only to the **single-line input** (where `multiline === false`), as a horizontal scroll clip
- The **multiline textarea** and its matching backdrop should use `overflow: 'visible'` (or simply omit the property) so the backdrop renders at full expanded height
- The outer wrapper `Box` (the one with the border) already clips visually, so removing overflow from the inner layers won't cause the field to break out of its container
