## Context

`src/react/pages/admin/EmailTemplatesPage.tsx` currently renders one flat table of all
templates ordered by key, with "Used by" handler chips driven by `HANDLER_EMAIL_TEMPLATES`
in `handlerMeta.ts`. That mapping is action-level only â€” nothing records **which outcome**
of an action sends which template (e.g. `open_deal_declined_thank_you` fires specifically on
the decline outcome of open_deal, and on deposit_invoice_followup's not-proceeding outcome).
Some templates are also sent from system modules outside card-action routes (`auth.js`,
`quickbooks.js`, `customer-info.js`, `photo-reviews.js`).

Goal: the page should read as "what emails does each action send, and on which outcome?" â€”
grouped accordions per action, outcome sub-groups inside, and a System emails group at the end.
